About Us

Tebonosi Fabrication & Logistics is a startup company that aspires to be the Apple, Amazon, and Facebook of fabrication and logistics. Founded with a vision of excellence, innovation, and precision, we are committed to redefining what modern fabrication and logistics can achieve.

We combine advanced fabrication capabilities with reliable logistics solutions to deliver a seamless, end‑to‑end experience for our customers. From custom builds to efficient transport coordination, our mission is to provide services that are not only dependable but transformative.

At Tebonosi Fabrication & Logistics, we believe that excellence is built through craftsmanship, integrity, and innovation. Every project we take on reflects our dedication to quality, our passion for problem‑solving, and our commitment to pushing the boundaries of what’s possible.
