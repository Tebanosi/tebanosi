About Us

Tebonosi Fabrication & Logistics is a startup company that aspires to be the Apple, Amazon, and Facebook of the logistics and fabrication world! Founded by Dr. Uvo Tebonosi Oghreikanone, we are driven by a bold vision: to redefine what efficiency, precision, and innovation look like in the modern industrial landscape.

We combine advanced fabrication capabilities with reliable logistics solutions to deliver a seamless, end‑to‑end experience for our clients. Whether it’s custom metalwork, structural fabrication, equipment repair, or coordinated transport, our mission is to provide services that are not only dependable but transformative.

At Tebonosi Fabrication & Logistics, we believe that excellence is built through craftsmanship, integrity, and forward‑thinking strategy. Our approach blends hands‑on expertise with a commitment to continuous improvement, ensuring that every project — big or small — meets the highest standards of quality.
